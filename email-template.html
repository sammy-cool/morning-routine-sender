**Your Morning Routine**

Start Your Day with a Positive Note!

Here's a simple morning routine to help you boost your productivity and set a positive tone for the day:

  • Gradual Wake-Up (9:00 AM - 9:10 AM)
  • Hydrate & Refresh (9:10 AM - 9:20 AM)
  • Energizing Yoga (9:20 AM - 9:40 AM)
  • Mindful Meditation (9:40 AM - 9:50 AM)
  • Light Breakfast (9:50 AM - 10:00 AM)
  • Set Daily Intentions (10:00 AM)

Have a great day!
